Who we are

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government, headquartered in Chhattisgarh. It holds 80G & 12A certifications ensuring transparency, accountability and tax-exempt benefits for donors. We are also CSR-1 and NITI Aayog registered, and hold the IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification — signifying our commitment to high-quality standards. With a strong network of volunteers and corporate partners, we work to bring positive change through food distribution, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental sustainability and social inclusion programs across India.

Why this work matters

Every cause we run exists because a gap was left unfilled — a meal missed, a child out of school, an animal with no one to call, a skill never taught. InAmigos exists to close that gap, one project and one volunteer at a time.
